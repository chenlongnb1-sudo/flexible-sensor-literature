# Biomimetic 3D Tactile Sensor System With Neuromorphic Encoding for Fascicle‐Level Feedback

- 期刊：Advanced Intelligent Systems
- 日期：2026-06-21
- DOI：10.1002/aisy.70458
- 解析状态：metadata_only_no_legal_pdf

## 摘要与研究价值

**Original:** Restoring tactile feedback that can be delivered through selective neural interfaces is essential for prosthetic embodiment. This study presents a biomimetic 3D tactile sensor system designed to convert skin‐like mechanical interactions into functionally selective neural stimulation patterns at the fascicle level. The system integrates a polydimethylsiloxane (PDMS)‐based artificial skin layer with tunable mechanical compliance, embedded slow‐adapting (SA) and fast‐adapting (FA) sensor channels, and a neuromorphic encoding framework that transforms analog sensor outputs into receptor‐potential‐like waveforms and action potential spike trains. MWCNT‐PDMS piezoresistive composites provide sustained pressure‐sensitive SA outputs, whereas BaTiO 3 ‐PDMS triboelectric composites provide transient dynamic‐event‐sensitive FA outputs. The size, footprint, and embedding depth of the sensors were selected to generate distinct receptive‐field‐inspired response profiles within the 3D skin matrix. In vivo validation using rat sciatic nerve stimulation showed that encoded pressing and tapping patterns elicited distinguishable electromyographic (EMG) responses in the tibialis anterior (TA) and gastrocnemius (GC) muscles. These results support the feasibility of a functionally selective biomimetic tactile front‐end for future closed‐loop neuroprosthetic feedback, while further device‐level durability, frequency‐response characterization, and real‐time integration remain necessary for clinical translation.

**中文:** 该工作构建仿生三维触觉前端，把皮肤式机械交互转换为可用于神经接口的选择性刺激模式。系统在可调顺应性的 PDMS 人工皮肤中嵌入慢适应（SA）和快适应（FA）通道：MWCNT-PDMS 压阻复合材料提供持续压力响应，BaTiO3-PDMS 摩擦电复合材料提供瞬态动态事件响应；随后用神经形态编码把模拟输出转换为类感受器电位波形和动作电位脉冲序列。传感单元尺寸、占地和埋入深度用于形成不同感受野，在大鼠坐骨神经刺激实验中，按压与敲击编码引起可区分的胫骨前肌和腓肠肌 EMG 响应。当前结论来自摘要级核验，尚未获得合法开放全文。

## 创新点

- 在同一三维 PDMS 皮肤中组合慢适应压阻通道与快适应摩擦电通道，分别编码持续压力和动态接触事件。
- 通过传感单元尺寸、占地和埋入深度塑造感受野响应，再把模拟信号转换为类感受器电位和脉冲序列。
- 把触觉前端输出连接到束级神经刺激，并用大鼠肌电响应验证按压与敲击模式具有功能选择性。

## 对当前课题的启发

- 把你的多通道前端按时间特性分工：持续分量负责法向压力，瞬态/差分分量负责敲击、滑移和纹理事件，再在 ADC 前组合为可解释特征。
- 将电极几何、微结构和嵌入深度作为“物理感受野”变量，系统比较感受野重叠、空间分辨率和装配偏移容忍度。
- 该研究的终点是神经刺激，不直接证明低冗余阵列或物理投影；可迁移的是 SA/FA 分工与前端编码框架。摘要也明确指出耐久性、频率响应和实时集成仍需验证。
