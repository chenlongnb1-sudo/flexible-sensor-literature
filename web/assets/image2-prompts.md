## Dashboard research visual

Use case: scientific-educational
Asset type: wide research dashboard header image for a flexible electronics laboratory application
Primary request: Create a high-fidelity realistic scientific visualization of a flexible electronic-skin tactile computing experiment. Show a thin translucent elastomer tactile sheet with a clearly visible 3 by 3 electrode array on a clean laboratory bench, connected by plausible copper traces and fine wires to a compact analog front-end circuit board with one ADC readout module. A precision robotic fingertip applies controlled lateral shear to the flexible surface. Use subtle non-text visual signal paths to suggest four local sensor responses combining into three directional vector channels and several spatial projection outputs.
Style/medium: credible editorial scientific product photography with restrained, physically grounded data-visualization accents
Composition/framing: wide 16:9 landscape, the full tactile sheet, fingertip, circuit board, and wiring clearly visible; balanced centered composition that survives a wide dashboard crop; no large empty areas
Lighting/mood: clean neutral laboratory lighting, precise and sophisticated, high material detail, no cinematic haze
Color palette: charcoal equipment, neutral white bench, translucent silicone, copper traces, restrained teal and amber signal accents
Constraints: no words, no labels, no numbers, no logos, no watermark, no human face, no fantasy components; physically plausible flexible electronics, wiring, instrumentation, and contact mechanics
Avoid: generic blue sci-fi interface, floating holograms, purple gradients, decorative bokeh, dark obscured subject, stock-photo look

Output: `tactile-front-end-image2.webp` (1672 x 941, converted from the selected PNG to WebP quality 88)

## App icon

Use case: logo-brand
Asset type: square application icon for a scientific research intelligence dashboard
Primary request: Create a compact scientific icon containing one four-quadrant tactile sensing pixel, three directional vector signal traces, and one analog summation node. Keep all components fully inside one compact square sensor body. The four tactile quadrants are the dominant central object. Three clean teal traces converge into a small amber circular computation node integrated inside the lower-right quadrant area. The amber node must be a plain glowing dot with no plus sign, cross, letters, numbers, or symbols.
Style/medium: polished flat 3D material icon with crisp geometry and subtle realistic depth, recognizable at 48 pixels
Composition/framing: centered square composition, main symbol fills about 72 percent of the canvas in both width and height, balanced even padding, one strong silhouette, no external wires extending beyond the square body, no outer app-store rounded frame
Lighting/mood: clean studio lighting, precise and modern
Color palette: charcoal sensor substrate, copper quadrant dividers, restrained teal vector traces, one amber computation dot, neutral off-white background
Constraints: no letters, no words, no numbers, no plus sign, no cross, no logo text, no watermark, no human hand, no brain symbol, no generic AI sparkles
Avoid: purple gradients, blue sci-fi glow, excessive circuit complexity, tiny decorative details, cartoon style, large empty margins

Output: `research-intelligence-icon-image2.png` (512 x 512, resized from the selected PNG with Lanczos resampling)
