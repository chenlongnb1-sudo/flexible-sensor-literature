# Image 2 asset prompts

## Dashboard research visual

Use case: scientific-educational
Asset type: research dashboard header image
Primary request: A high-fidelity editorial scientific visualization of a flexible electronic-skin tactile computing experiment. Show a thin translucent elastomer tactile sheet with a visible 3x3 electrode array, connected to a compact analog front-end circuit board and one ADC readout, while a robotic fingertip applies controlled shear to the surface. Subtle physical signal paths should visually connect the sensor quadrants to three vector channels and spatial projection features.
Style/medium: realistic scientific product photography with restrained data-visualization overlays
Composition/framing: wide 16:9 landscape, equipment centered-right, enough quiet dark negative space on the left for dashboard copy
Lighting/mood: clean laboratory lighting, precise and credible, no cinematic haze
Color palette: charcoal, neutral white, copper traces, restrained teal and amber signal accents
Constraints: no text, no labels, no logos, no watermark, no fantasy components, no human face, show physically plausible flexible electronics and bench instrumentation
Avoid: generic blue sci-fi interface, floating holograms, purple gradients, decorative bokeh, stock-photo look
